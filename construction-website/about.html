About BTH Construction

Learn about our history, values, and commitment to excellence in construction.

Our Story

Founded in 2008, BTH Construction has grown from a small local contractor to a respected construction company serving clients throughout the region. Our journey began with a simple mission: to deliver quality construction services with integrity and professionalism.

Our Mission

To build exceptional spaces that enhance lives and communities through innovative design, quality craftsmanship, and unwavering commitment to client satisfaction.

Our Values

  • Quality: We never compromise on materials or workmanship
  • Integrity: We conduct business with honesty and transparency
  • Safety: We prioritize the well-being of our team and clients
  • Innovation: We embrace new technologies and methods
  • Community: We give back to the communities we serve
